Artist: Andrew Rollins

Curator: Maitha

Artwork: CONVERSION 3 Collage (Top) / HOMOMORMON 4 (Bottom)

Description: The CONVERSION series addresses the artist’s 4 years of conversion therapy in preparation for his 2 year Missionary service as a Mormon Missionary. Utilizing the historically loaded material of personal classified ads in gay publications from the 80’s. The classified ads, a platform designed to maintain discreteness and to conceal identity; being paired with words or “Labels” in white as a way to cover up and distract the viewer’s eye from seeing what’s underneath. Ultimately unsuccessful, the background is still visible.

The HOMOMORMON series addresses the artist’s 4 years of conversion therapy in preparation for his 2 year Missionary service as a Mormon Missionary. The artist confronts the conflict between their identities: Homosexual man and a fifth generation Mormon. Illustrating the physical and mental struggle with this dichotomy of identity, looking for a solution or at least a place of compromise.
