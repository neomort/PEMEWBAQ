Artist: Reddit User "SnooWoofers3515"

Curator: Sarah

Artwork: Untitled Work by Reddit User SnooWoofers3515

Medium: Web Art

Description quoted from the artist's comment: “The pieces in this triptych are as follows: I am Judith, I am Lucrezia (oil on canvas) Lamentation of the Former Self (mounted on frame charcoal, Conte crayon, and pastel drawing) Solitary Transfiguration (oil on canvas) They're pretty large (the middle is 30"×44") and the most ambitious thing I've done yet. Each references famous Christian mythological paintings from the middle ages (the titles are meant to help inform which). Feel free to ask questions about any symbolism!”
