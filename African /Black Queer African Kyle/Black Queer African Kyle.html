Context: The nest collective is a Nairobi-based multidisciplinary artist collective. In 2014, they published Stories of Our Lives, the culmination of a research project that began as a way of documenting the stories persons identifying as gay, lesbian, bisexual, transgender and intersex from Kenya. These stories were eventually turned into both a book and a series of short films. This concept album consists of four songs featured in the film.
