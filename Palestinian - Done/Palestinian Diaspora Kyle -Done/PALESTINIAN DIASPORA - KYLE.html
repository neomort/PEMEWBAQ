Artist: Ahlam Shibli احلام شبلي

Curator: Kyle

Artwork: Voyage in mt. Tabor

Medium: Photographic study

Date: 1998

Context: This work by Palistinean artist, Ahlam Shibli, is a mysterious work without an artist statement, consisting of 9 dreamlike images that seem to archive the artist’s trip to mt. Tabor in the late 90s. Shibli’s other works depict both the tragedy and beauty in the Palistinean experience through an intimate perspective featuring documentary aesthetics. This work felt to me like it was a reflection of collective memory among Palistinean diaspora centered in a dreamlike place that is both vivid and timeless.
